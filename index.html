About Me

🎓 Academic Background

After completing F.Sc, I cleared multiple entrance exams — NUST, Punjab University, and NTU — and secured admission at UET Faisalabad. My cousin joined alongside me, and together we started this academic journey. I commuted daily as a Day Scholar before my family relocated to Faisalabad, turning saved travel time into more study and gym sessions.

💡 How I Learn

I treat every subject like a sport: consistent practice beats cramming. From scoring full marks in Calculus mids to conquering Discrete Mathematics through marathon library sessions, I believe no subject is impossible with the right mindset. I apply the same discipline to code that I apply to my daily mile run.

I run 1 mile every day and train regularly at the gym. Badminton sharpens my reflexes and keeps me grounded. Physical fitness is not separate from academic performance — it is the engine behind it. These habits helped me manage long commutes, exam stress, and increasing workloads throughout Semester 1.

🔭 What I'm Building Toward

In Semester 2, I'm diving deep into Object-Oriented Programming with C#, Database Systems, Applied Statistics, and Communication Skills. I'm aiming to surpass my 3.79 CGPA while exploring more real-world applications of the concepts I'm learning — especially in data and software engineering.
