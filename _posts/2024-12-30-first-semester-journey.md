# My First Semester at UET Faisalabad 🎓

After completing F.Sc, I appeared in multiple entrance exams including 
NUST, Punjab University, and NTU simultaneously. After securing admission 
in both NTU and UET Faisalabad, I chose UET Faisalabad alongside my cousin 
and we started this academic journey together.

## Life as a Day Scholar

I started as a Day Scholar, commuting via public transport to Abdullah Pur 
bus stop daily. To stay energized through the long commute days, I began 
running 1 mile every morning before leaving home. Mid semester, my family 
relocated to Faisalabad which freed up my travel time for deeper study 
sessions and more intensive gym workouts — a major turning point in my 
academic performance.

## My Semester 1 Results

I finished Semester 1 with a CGPA of 3.79 out of 4.0. Here are my results:

| Subject | Grade |
|---|---|
| Fehm-ul-Quran | A+ |
| Calculus I | A |
| ICT Lab | A |
| Applied Physics | A− |
| Discrete Mathematics | A− |
| PF Lab | A− |
| Physics Lab | A− |
| Programming Fundamentals | B+ |

## Key Highlights

I scored a perfect mid-term score in Calculus I through consistent daily 
practice, treating it like an athletic training routine with no skipped 
reps. In Discrete Mathematics, I conquered the subject through marathon 
library sessions, proving that no subject is impossible with the right 
mindset. For Programming Fundamentals, I built a USA Car Price Predictor 
using Machine Learning which was praised by our instructor.

## Lessons Learned

Semester 1 taught me that consistency beats cramming every single time. 
The same discipline I apply to my daily mile run, I applied to my studies 
and it paid off with a 3.79 CGPA. My target for Semester 2 is to surpass 
this result.

> "Consistent practice beats cramming. No subject is impossible 
> with the right mindset."
