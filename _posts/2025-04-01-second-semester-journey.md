# My Second Semester at UET Faisalabad 🎓

After achieving a 3.79 CGPA in Semester 1, I entered Semester 2 with 
a clear target — to surpass my previous result and grow both academically 
and personally. This semester brought new challenges, new subjects, and 
new opportunities to level up.

## Subjects This Semester

In Semester 2, I am studying Object-Oriented Programming with C#, 
Database Systems, Applied Statistics, and Communication Skills based 
on the 7 Cs of Communication. Each subject is pushing me to think 
differently and apply knowledge in real world scenarios.

## My Approach

I treat every subject like a sport — consistent practice beats cramming 
every single time. For OOP with C#, I practice coding daily. For Database 
Systems, I am building real queries and understanding how data is stored 
and managed. For Applied Statistics, I am connecting mathematical concepts 
to real world data analysis.

## Challenges Faced

This semester had online mid-term exams due to a fuel crisis which was 
a unique challenge. Despite this, I stayed focused and maintained my 
study routine. I am returning to campus on April 1st and looking forward 
to in-person classes and lab sessions again.

## Goals for Semester 2

My primary goal is to surpass my 3.79 CGPA from Semester 1. I am also 
exploring more real world applications of the concepts I am learning, 
especially in data and software engineering. I am still running daily, 
still coding daily, and never stopping learning.

> "Level up every semester. Same discipline, higher targets."
