A few of the posts on this site reference **Dr. Bilal Ahmad**, who taught me Programming Fundamentals and Database Systems during this semester at UET Lahore (Faisalabad Campus). He works in AI, ML, and deep learning model training, and often encouraged us to work with real-world datasets — particularly medical datasets — because of how precisely they're collected and how much practical value they carry.

It was Dr. Ahmad's suggestion to document my coursework publicly that led to this portfolio in the first place, so it felt right to credit him here rather than scatter the links across every post.
